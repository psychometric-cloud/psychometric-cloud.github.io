Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free version available forever?
Yes. The free plan is yours forever. It includes all the core features but with some limitations. We created it so you can try the app before purchasing, and only upgrade when you feel confident it’s right for you.
Do I have to pay a commission on lessons?
No. Apart from your subscription fee, you don’t owe anything else. In other words, your NextTutor fees won’t increase if you teach more lessons.
Does the subscription renew automatically?
Yes. The subscription renews automatically. You can cancel anytime.
Can I cancel the subscription?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time.
What happens after cancelling the subscription?
Cancellation does not take effect immediately—it simply stops future renewals. This means you can continue using the system normally until your current subscription period ends. You can also reverse your cancellation request anytime.
Is there a limit to the number of students?
Yes. Currently, the maximum number of students is 50. Soon, we will increase the limit to 100 students.
Is there any limit for the amount of lessons/assignments I can give?
No. There is no limit on the total number of lessons you can give. However, there is a technical limit of up to 10 lessons per day.
