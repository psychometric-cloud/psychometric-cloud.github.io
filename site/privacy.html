Privacy Policy
Last update: July 13th, 2024

NextTutor Ltd. ("NextTutor", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website, mobile app, and services (collectively, the “Services”).

By using NextTutor, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

1. Information We Collect:

1.1 Information You Provide:

When you use our Services, you may provide us with:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile information (e.g., bio, teaching subjects, photo – optional)
  • Communications via the platform (e.g., chat, files)
  • Payment-related information (processed by third parties)
  • Role information (e.g., student or tutor)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We may collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and general location (not precise)
  • Platform usage data (e.g., features used, session durations)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive data from:

  • Payment processors (e.g., transaction status)
  • Identity verification providers (if used in the future)

We do not collect sensitive biometric, health, or precise geolocation data.

2. How We Use Your Information:

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the platform
  • Enable users to find and contact each other independently
  • Facilitate communication and payments between users
  • Provide support and respond to user requests
  • Improve security, performance, and reliability
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not match or recommend specific tutors or students. All interactions are initiated voluntarily by the users.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with:

  • Service Providers – such as hosting, analytics, email, and payment processing providers
  • Other Users – basic public profile data is visible to other users (e.g., name, subject taught)
  • Legal or Regulatory Authorities – if required by applicable law or legal process
  • In the Event of a Merger or Sale – user data may be transferred with the business, with notice

We require third parties to handle your data responsibly and only for intended purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the Services
  • Comply with legal and accounting obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time, subject to applicable laws.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Access controls
  • Secure storage of user credentials (e.g., hashed passwords)

While we do our best to secure your data, no system is entirely immune from risk.

6. Your Rights:

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)

To exercise your rights, contact us at:

privacy@nexttutor.app

We may ask for verification to protect your privacy.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Services are intended for users aged 13 and older. If we learn we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

We may process data on servers located outside your country of residence. By using the Services, you consent to such transfers in accordance with this Policy and applicable law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice or email. Continued use of the Services after such updates indicates your acceptance.

10. Contact Us:

  • For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact:

    support@nexttutor.io.
