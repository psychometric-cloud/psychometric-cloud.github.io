Track Your Income.
No more digging through notes or guessing who paid when. With NextTutor, all deposits are organized in one place - just a few clicks away.
Easy View
Every deposit made by your students—whether through PayPal or any other provider—is automatically recorded in our system, so you can view them all in one convenient place. You can easily filter deposits by a specific student or by a selected date range.
Smart Insights
Tracking your income daily is important — but seeing the big picture is just as crucial. That’s why NextTutor summarizes your earnings by week and month, helping you identify patterns and trends with ease.
